My Game Demos

Dark Spells (September 2021)

This is the first game project I participated. I worked on enemy design and engineering, map design, and sound system.

This game is a 2D soul-like game, and with a lore based on Touhou Project. The main character wakes up in the graveyard knowing nothing about herself. Through exploring the dangerous world, she gradually gets to realize the mystery about herself and the approaching disaster.

Since the game producer is still developing this game, I may not be able to provide a playable demo. Hope to see this game soon :)

Sweep Jumping mine (July 2021)

This is the game demo I made with another programmer in 2021 CiGA Gamejam. It was the first time for me to join a gamejam. I worked on game design and part of programming.

This game is a combination of Chinese checkers and Mine Sweeping. In this game, two players need to move their mines and defuse opponent's mines.

Without any artists, this game isn't very attractive. This taught me an important lesson: do not hesitate to cooperate.

Cyber Magpie Bridge (May 2022)

This is a game I made with two friends in a local gamejam. I worked as game designer, and programmer.

In this game, player runs a dating agency and needs to choose the best partner for the customers. The better couple is made, the more player earns. And certain couple may have certain effect to the continuing story.

View it at itch.io

Halloween Trick (June 2022)

This is a 2D vampire-survivor like game made for 2022 CiGA Gamejam. In this gamejam, I led a group of three, and worked as game designer and engineer.

In this game, player needs to treat the incoming kids with the candy. Every kid satisfied gives player exp, and player gets a random upgrade from the base when level up. After the end of each night, player can buy upgrades or remove the upgrade in the base.

In Vampire Survivor, player needs to keep moving around randomly to stay alive. However in this game, I created two defensive towers to encourage player to make a more strategic move. Since these towers require players to stay close, or they will run out of power in a short time.

View it at itch.io

Run in maze (December 2022)

This game is made for CIS 2022 online program. I made this game with three teammates. I worked as game designer, programmer, and made the input system, AI pathfinding algorithm, maze generation, fog system and vision system.

In this game, player needs to explore the unknown maze, collect the lost treasure, escape from the monster, and find the stairs to the next floor.

This game is done with pygame on python, which doesn't have much plug-and-play function. Therefore, I made a simple engine for teammates to use. This made our development fast and smooth.
